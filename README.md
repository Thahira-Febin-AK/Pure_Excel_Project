## üìä Dashboard Insights
### Age Group Analysis
- Adults (478 purchases) are the largest segment, followed by Young buyers (265).  
- Seniors contribute minimally (50).  
**Insight:** Adults and Young customers are the most promising target groups for marketing campaigns.  

### Income Level Impact
- Low Income (493) and Lower Middle Income (425) groups dominate bike purchases.  
- High Income buyers are very few (31).  
**Insight:** Affordability strongly influences bike purchases. Budget and mid‚Äërange models should be prioritized.  

### Regional Sales
- Europe records the highest bike sales, particularly among lower‚Äëmiddle income customers.  
- North America and Pacific regions show balanced but smaller contributions.

## ‚úÖ Conclusion & Business Recommendations
Bike purchases are driven by Adults and Young customers, primarily from low to middle income groups, with strong representation from educated professionals. Commute distance patterns show bikes are favored for short trips, and marital status analysis reveals balanced demand across both segments.  

**Recommendations:**
