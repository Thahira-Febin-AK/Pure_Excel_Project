## üìä Dashboard Insights

### Age Group Analysis
- Adults (478 purchases) are the largest segment, followed by Young buyers (265).  
- Seniors contribute minimally (50).  
**Insight:** Adults and Young customers are the most promising target groups for marketing campaigns.  

### Income Level Impact
- Low Income (493) and Lower Middle Income (425) groups dominate bike purchases.  
- High Income buyers are very few (31).  
**Insight:** Affordability strongly influences bike purchases. Budget and mid‚Äërange models should be prioritized.  

### Regional Sales
- Europe records the highest bike sales, especially among lower‚Äëmiddle income customers.  
- North America and Pacific regions show balanced but smaller contributions.  
**Insight:** Europe is the strongest market, while North America offers lifestyle‚Äëdriven opportunities.  

### Commute Distance
- Short commutes (0‚Äì1 miles) drive the majority of purchases.

### Marital Status & Gender

### Education
- Bachelors (311) and Graduate Degree holders (278) dominate purchases.  
**Insight:** Educated professionals are a strong aspirational segment for targeted campaigns.  

### Home Ownership & Car Ownership

## ‚úÖ Conclusion & Business Recommendations

**Recommendations:**
- **Target Marketing:** Focus campaigns on Adults and Young professionals with affordable models.

- **Commute Positioning:** Market bikes as convenient short‚Äëdistance transport.

- **Education‚ÄëDriven Campaigns:** Position bikes as aspirational products for graduates and young professionals.
